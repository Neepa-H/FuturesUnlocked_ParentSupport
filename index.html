Other Pathways

Some students choose routes that are not shown in the main grid but can be equally valuable. For example, supported internships and supported apprenticeships offer structured help for young people with Education, Health and Care Plans (EHCPs) or additional needs. These include job coaching and a strong focus on building independence and employability. Some students join the Armed Forces, where training, qualifications and career development are combined with service. Entry-level work or sector-based training programmes may also help young people build skills and move into apprenticeships or further study.
